Poem de fortnite

You priceless free application,
allowed me to vegetate for hours at ease.
You innovative approach to gaming,
that will forever own a five star suite in my heart.
Some hear your name,
and chuckle ahaha or take part in mockery.
good golly do I feel bad,
at how they themselves must miss your gameplay.
From Haunted Hills to Lucky Landings,
you gifted me a virtual world of possibilities filled with joy.
Combining creativity with competitiveness,
a victory royale, Nirvana is what I would see.
You lost your unmatched popularity,
and like unrefrigerated milk, you drew sour.
It hurts me to even say it,
but I resented you and cut you out of my life.
Everything turned to grey
until I rediscovered my love for you
You aged not like yogurt but like wine! and I’d take criticism from others to keep our spark alive.
I love how you pushed me to live to my fullest. I love how you unlock my societal chains. I love that you will forever be a part of me.
I love you Fortnite.
